Who I Am

Hi, I'm Sujin. Ever since I was young, I have been passionate about design. So I'm currently in the design department.

Among them, I became interested in designing web/app pages and developing UI. It is a big task for me to do a user-centered design that fits the web standard and is easy for anyone to use. Also, it is my goal and direction that I want to go to to to keep the code concise without being too long in the UI development part.

In addition, UI/UX design sometimes requires static design, but it is fun to do dynamic design on pages frequently used by young people. Because of that, I became interested in motion graphics.

This site wants to display the tasks I have done so far and the works I have done. Thank you for reading it!
