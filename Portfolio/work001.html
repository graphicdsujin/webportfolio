Project Details

Project01. Connectivity

Overview

I always strive to provide the right experience and to provide an experience to effectively sell and convey your story. Repeated and collaborative processes help define goals and KPIs, and there is a reason to make decisions. My site, based on Webflow with reason, lays the foundation for achieving long-term business goals, but will always be flexible to meet today's changing needs.

Workflow

The concept of connecting digital and analog through interaction is diverse because it has a wide range. In my first concept note, I added ideas for content.(Fig 11) I drew simple ideas such as dots and faces, vectors and bitmaps, real and virtual, identification and anonymity, humans and machines. And I was thinking it would be good to combine some of them into a single web page.

Testing & Iteration

In order to bind five contents with a small concept, a ".card" class was added and added to five elements to minimize duplication. And each element was piled up and placed using Position:absolute. JavaScript is not allowed for the task, so I can use the mouse over function as much as possible to give animation effects within css. Drop shadow, spin, mouseover and many effects were used in this web site.
